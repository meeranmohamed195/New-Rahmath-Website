WHO WE ARE

  • Celebrating Over 14 Years of Tradition and Culinary Excellence.
  • Unwavering in our commitment to quality.

A haven of flavors with a legacy spanning over 14 years. Rooted in the time-honored recipes handed down through generations, our chefs passionately channel the spirit of our founder's grandfather. The distinctive aroma of our biryanis and gravies wafts through our kitchen, where a firewood stove imparts a unique character. Our commitment to sourcing the finest ingredients ensures a dining experience that transcends the ordinary.
